Varsity Basketball Management

Working Behind the Table

As a manager, one of my main jobs is working at the scorers table where I must utilize quick reactions and quick critical thinking to keep score and time of rapidly moving and changing Varsity Basketball games.

Photo

Aiding the Team

As a manager, on top of working behind the table, of course, the most critical part of my role is to help the players and the team whenever it's needed. I must predict patterns be able to analyze critical, and sometimes, subtle bodily cues provided by the players and coaches to help them with whatever they need so that my team can function at it's best.
